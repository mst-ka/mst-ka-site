Apply Online

At Kappa Alpha Order we are determined to recruit men who will become contributing members to our brotherhood. This application is meant to help us get to know you better and decide whether you have what it takes to become a member of Kappa Alpha. We ask that you fill this out honestly because properly picking members is just as important for you as it is for us.


Every summer we host several Rush events in the St. Louis and Kansas City areas. These events are meant to help us get to know you better, and for you to get to know us better. It is not required for you to attend a Rush event, but it is highly recommended that you do so. All costs of the events are covered by KA and rides to and from the events can be arranged upon request. For more information or to sign up for an event please contact either one of our Recruitment Chairs.
